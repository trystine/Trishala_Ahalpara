# Tell Me: An LLM-powered Mental Well-being Assistant

- **Project Years:** Jan 2025 – Jun 2026
- **Project Type:** Personal Research Project · [GitHub](https://github.com/trystine)

**Description of project:**

Tell Me is a multi-component generative AI platform for mental well-being that integrates retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), synthetic dialogue generation, and agentic planning workflows.

- Built a **personalized RAG-based conversational assistant** using LlamaIndex and local LLMs to deliver context-aware, knowledge-grounded well-being support while reducing hallucinations through retrieval-based reasoning.
- Developed a **synthetic client–therapist dialogue generation framework** conditioned on user profiles to create privacy-preserving datasets for therapeutic language research and data augmentation.
- Implemented a **multi-agent Well-being AI Crew** using CrewAI and LangGraph to generate adaptive weekly self-care plans, guided meditation content, and personalized well-being recommendations.
- Designed and executed an **evaluation framework** combining LLM-as-a-Judge methodologies and human-user studies to assess response quality, relevance, and user satisfaction.

## Technologies Used

Python, LLMs, RAG, LlamaIndex, CrewAI, LangGraph, Ollama, NLP

## Project Outcome

Authored and presented a research paper accepted at the **2nd International Conference on Sustainability, Innovation, and Society (ICSIS 2026)**.
