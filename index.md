# Bio

I am an **Applied AI Research Engineer** at **Fujitsu Research of America**, where I develop enterprise AI systems that bridge cutting-edge research and real-world applications. My work focuses on **Large Language Models (LLMs)**, **Agentic AI**, **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)**, **Process Intelligence**, and **Human-Centered AI**, with an emphasis on building scalable, trustworthy, and impactful intelligent systems.

My research interests lie at the intersection of **generative AI**, **machine learning**, and **decision intelligence**. I enjoy designing AI systems that combine reasoning, retrieval, planning, and data-driven learning to solve complex business and societal challenges. My work spans research, system development, evaluation, and deployment, with contributions to enterprise AI solutions, peer-reviewed publications, and AI innovation.

I'm deeply immersed in building and evaluating AI systems that turn complex data into actionable insights. Here are a few areas where I shine:
