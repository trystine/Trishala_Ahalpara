## 🔮 Tell Me: Mental Well-being AI Platform

I'm actively developing **[Tell Me](projects/tell_me.md)**, a multi-component generative AI platform for mental well-being:

- **RAG-based conversational assistant** using LlamaIndex and local LLMs for context-aware, knowledge-grounded support
- **Synthetic client–therapist dialogue generation** for privacy-preserving therapeutic language research
- **Multi-agent Well-being AI Crew** (CrewAI + LangGraph) for adaptive weekly self-care plans and personalized recommendations
- **Evaluation framework** combining LLM-as-a-Judge methodologies and human-user studies

This work was accepted as a research paper at **ICSIS 2026** (2nd International Conference on Sustainability, Innovation, and Society).
